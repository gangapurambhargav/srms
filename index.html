About the University

Malla Reddy engineering College (MREC) is the parent college of the Malla Reddy Group of Institutions, Hyderabad, Telangana, India founded by Shri Ch Malla Reddy.The institute was established in 2002, is approved by the AICTE New Delhi, and was affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH). In 2008 the college was accredited by NBA.The college has been certified by NAAC as an A grade institution in the Hyderabad region.[1] The college has been granted Permanent Affiliation, and Autonomous status by JNTU in 2011..
